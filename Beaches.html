Popular Beaches









Glen Beach

Glen Beach is located north of Camps Bay Beach in Cape Town, South Africa. The two beaches are divided by a rock outcrop that extends from the land to the shore-line.

  • Address: Tafelberg Road, Cape Town, 8000

  • Area: 221 km2

  • Hours: 09:00 - 17:00 daily

  • Established: 19 May 1998



Muizenberg Beach

Known for its brightly painted beach huts, this escape from the city is popular with surfers.

  • Address: Beach Road, Muizenberg, Cape Town, 7950

  • Hours: Open 24 Hours



Boulders Beach

Boulders Beach is a sheltered beach made up of inlets between granite boulders, from which the name originated. It is located in the Cape Peninsula, near Simon's Town towards Cape Point, near Cape Town in the Western Cape province of South Africa. It is also commonly known as Boulders Bay.

  • Address: Kleintuin Road

  • Hours: Open 24 Hours
